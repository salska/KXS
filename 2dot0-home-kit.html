Home Kit

The home network provides the connectivity between key home kit devices and provides access to essential NAS Device hosted services including Music Streaming, Video Streaming, Books Library and Container Stack.

Hence, it's critical that the Home Network performance is monitored and fine tuned to maximise througput by reducing latency and utilising the most efficient network settings.
