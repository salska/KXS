Reducing theRTT over wifi is achieved by changing the frequency band and allocated channels to see if these make a big difference to the observed latency during a sustained ping test

Using the NetgearOrbi setup the device supports 2.5GHz (channel range 1 - 13) and 5GHz (36, 40, 44 & 48) bandwidths and can hop through a number of channels to improve throughput and bandwidth.

A simple test was undertaken to find the best ping timings for a choice of 5GHz channels and the optimum appeared to be Channel 40. This was held constant and the 2.5G channels varied and timings measured.

See 2.6.2 Home network bandwidth settings for details of the results.
